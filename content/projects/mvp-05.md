**What it is:**  
A transcript-first editing flow that lets you create clean clips without dragging timeline handles.

**How it works:**
1. Upload or import a long recording.
2. Auto-transcribe it.
3. Highlight the lines you want to keep.
4. Export a clip (or a batch of clips) with captions.

**Why it matters:**  
It turns the slowest part of editing—scrubbing and trimming—into a fast text-selection workflow.
