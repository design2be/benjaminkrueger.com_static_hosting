**What it is:**  
A simple workflow for generating upgraded photos that still look like you.

**User flow:**
1. Upload a small set of photos.
2. Choose a preset/style that matches your vibe.
3. Generate multiple photo options and pick your favorites for dating, social, or LinkedIn.

**Why it works:**  
The product focuses on practical outcomes (more matches, a cleaner feed, more credibility) while keeping results recognizable and consistent across platforms.
