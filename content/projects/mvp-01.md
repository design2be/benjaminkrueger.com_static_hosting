**MVP idea (auto-insert scope):**  
Build a lightweight browser extension that detects when a user focuses a comment field, opens a small popover with a few reply-style options, generates 3–5 context-aware suggestions, and pastes the selected suggestion directly into the active input field.

**User flow:**
1. User clicks the comment/reply field they want to respond to.
2. A popover appears next to the field with reply-style buttons and suggested replies.
3. The user clicks a suggestion and it’s automatically inserted into the comment field (editable before posting).

The tool removes the friction of figuring out what to say by instantly turning context into ready-to-post replies, saving time, reducing social anxiety, and helping users consistently respond with the right tone—whether they want to be thoughtful, witty, professional, or diplomatic.
